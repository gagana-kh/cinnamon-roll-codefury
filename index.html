ONLINE HARASSMENT: An abusive behavior that happens online through various platforms. It is repeat unsolicited and abusive contact, with the goal of intimidating or frightening the target. Online harassment can include name-calling, stalking, physical threats, and humiliation. It can also have an offline component, where the harassment starts or escalates offline. Online harassment can make the target feel unsafe, humiliated, scared or emotionally distressed. Most common forms of online harassment are Trolling , threats about physical harm and sexual harassment, cyber stalking, doxing , character assassinations, cyberbullying.Online harassment is a serious issue that can have a significant impact on an individual’s mental and emotional well-being. ANGER ISSUES: Anger issues refer to difficulties in managing and controlling anger in a healthy and constructive manner. People with anger issues may experience intense and frequent anger that disrupts their daily life, relationships, and overall well-being.It's important to recognize that anger issues are treatable, and seeking help is a proactive step towards managing and improving one's emotional well-being. A mental health professional can provide personalized guidance and support tailored to an individual's specific situation and needs. To know more

ANXIETY AND PANIC ATTACK: Anxiety and panic attacks are two related but distinct mental health experiences. Anxiety is a normal and adaptive emotion that helps us respond to threats and stressful situations. However, when anxiety becomes excessive, chronic, or overwhelming, it can develop into an anxiety disorder.A panic attack is a sudden and intense episode of fear or anxiety that can occur without warning. Panic attacks are characterized by a rapid onset of severe physical and emotional symptoms.It's important to note that while anxiety and panic attacks can be distressing, they are treatable conditions.
